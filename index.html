About BCA

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is a three-year undergraduate degree in computer science. It teaches programming, databases, software development, networking, and essential IT skills. After completing BCA, students can build careers in software development, web development, data analysis, and other IT fields or continue higher studies like MCA or MBA.
